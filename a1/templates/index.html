Our Motivation

We believe that it is important to not waste food as much as possible. Food donation has been increasing for the past few decades as the awareness for the lack of food for the underprivileged has increased. Big stores, restaurants, grocery shops, etc. are also frequently donating leftover food or supplies to the food banks, food pantries and local food establishments.

We are just doing our part by making food donation easier for the common populace.
